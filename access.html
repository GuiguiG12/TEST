How deposits, protection and withdrawals work for Flex and Secure.

Once approved, you'll receive a unique deposit address. You can send USDC via the Base or Ethereum network directly from Coinbase, Revolut, or your wallet.

We offer downside protection: G12 Labs commits to covering trading losses up to a defined threshold so your principal is preserved at maturity, assuming no counterparty or protocol failure.

For the Flex Fund, you can redeem with 48h notice. For the Secure Fund, capital is locked for 12 months, but your yield is paid out monthly.
