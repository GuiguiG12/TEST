Roadmap

Built in phases, with deliberate constraints.

The objective is not speed, but robustness. Each phase must be validated before expanding the scope.

Phase 1
Completed
Foundations & early research

Building the core structure of the fund: rule-based strategies, risk budgets, datasets, and early simulations. Defining the system architecture and establishing the theoretical backbone.

Phase 2
Completed
Model development & infrastructure build-out

Developing the strategy suite and assembling execution, reporting, and risk-monitoring infrastructure. Integrating operational components required for controlled access and long-term reliability.

Phase 3
Now
Private pilot & live testing

Running the system with a select group of early partners through a monitored flow in real market conditions. Refining models, enforcing capital protection rules, and maintaining transparent tracking.

Phase 4
Planned
Scaled deployment

Increasing capacity, formalising onboarding mechanisms, and expanding automated reporting. Structuring the fund for disciplined scale-up and sustainable growth.

Phase 5
Exploratory
Vault architecture & external backing

Exploring tokenisation, vault structures, and potential on-chain accounting solutions. Engaging with external backers to support expansion and long-term positioning.

FAQ

Questions partners usually ask.

G12 Labs is an experimental crypto lab running systematic, data-driven strategies in real market conditions. The pilot is funded by the founders and a small circle of partners – it is not an exchange or a public investment product.

We run multiple rule-based strategies that focus on repeatable edges: funding-rate capture, directional or market-neutral systems, and liquidity-providing setups. Everything is tracked over time so we can see how they behave across different market regimes.

There is always risk: capital can go down as well as up. We manage this with position sizing rules, drawdown thresholds, diversification across strategies, and a strong bias toward capital preservation before performance.

The pilot is currently open. To join, just follow the instructions in the “Private Pilot Raise” section and create your account on our platform. Once your account is created, our team will send you a personal MEXC identifier by email — this ID is required to deposit funds into the lab’s vaults and track allocations securely.
